Harbours of Hope: Mediterranean Migration Crisis

Welcome to the Harbours of Hope Project

Our project, undertaken by a group of students, seeks to deepen comprehension of the Mediterranean migration crisis by visualizing migrant youth-related data. Through interactive charts and graphs, we endeavor to illuminate their challenges and triumphs amid arduous journeys. This project is part of our coursework in Interactive Information Visualization, and our main goal is to contribute meaningfully to the understanding of migration dynamics.

About the Visualizations

Our visualizations provide a comparative analysis of educational achievements and employment statuses among young migrants across various EU countries. They illuminate disparities and commonalities, shedding light on the diverse outcomes resulting from migrant integration policies. Serving as a valuable resource, these visualizations cater to researchers, humanitarian organizations, policymakers, and socially conscious individuals, fostering informed decision-making and engagement. Understanding these patterns is pivotal in addressing the educational and employment challenges faced by migrant youth, driving the development of more effective support systems and inclusive policies. Users can navigate the visualizations by selecting specific years, with flags representing different EU countries and their placement indicating key education and employment statistics.
